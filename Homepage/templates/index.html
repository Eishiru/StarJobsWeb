About Us

Starjobs allows JOBSTERS (independent contractors, freelancers, relievers) connect to clients and principals that offer short-term and temporary gig engagement

  • Flexibility: Get immediate talent or skills for augmentation and reinforcement (screened and filtered) through reviews.

  • Freedom: Control of additional hours (calibrated per hour).

  • Cost-efficiency: Savings and secured with fidelity CGLI and Accident Insurances.

  • Ratings and Reviews: Posting of reviews and ratings on JOBSTERS (Independent Contractor or Freelancer or reliever) performance.

Vision

To be the top software application for the gig economy in Southeast Asia.

Mission

To allow the JOBSTERS (independent contractors, freelancers, and relievers) and CLIENTS (principals):

  • Get the freedom to CONNECT and create your gig schedule

  • ENGAGE to thousands of gigs available

  • ACCELERATE profit and career
